## Usage Guidelines
1. Each page keeps one primary keyword (Chinese now; English later if multilingual).
2. Insert primary keyword in: H1, first 100 chars, one H2, alt text (descriptive, no stuffing).
3. Use 2–3 secondary variations naturally; avoid repetitive stuffing.
4. Internal links: at least 2 pointing to related service or case pages with varied anchor text.
5. Update this mapping when new services added; maintain uniqueness.
